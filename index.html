About me

I am a Computer Science student, studying at the Technical University of Munich since 2021 where I put my focus on Machine Learning, Software Engineering and Molecular Dynamics.
I am currently working on my Bachelor Thesis in the field of Molecular Dynamics, Molecular dynamics is a powerful tool for studying the behavior of molecules and e.g. used for drug discovery. I am researching ways to use Data Science to speed up molecular dynamics simulations, and to improve the accuracy of the simulations.
Furthermore I was an exchange student at the University of California Davis, UC Davis which is located near Silicon Valley is renowned in the US for its dynamic approach to fostering technological innovation and academic excellence. At UC Davis I took classes in economics, which is my minor and in advanced topics of computer science and in political science to broaden my horizon how different political systems work.
During my studies, I concentrated my elective coursework on Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence because I recognized their pivotal role in driving technological innovation and transforming approaches to solving complex real-world challenges.
