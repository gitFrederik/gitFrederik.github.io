About me

I am a Computer Science student, studying at the Technical University of Munich since 2021 where I put my focus on Machine Learning, Software Engineering and Molecular Dynamics.
I am currently working on my Bachelor Thesis in the field of Molecular Dynamics, Molecular dynamics is a powerful tool for studying the behavior of molecules and e.g. used for drug discovery. I am researching ways to leverage Data Science and Machine Learning methods to speed up molecular dynamics simulations, and improve the accuracy of the simulations.
Furthermore I was an exchange student at the University of California Davis, UC Davis which is located near Silicon Valley is renowned in the US for its dynamic approach to fostering technological innovation and academic excellence. I expanded my perspective through advanced coursework in Computer Science, Economics (my minor), and Political Science. These experiences have equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of global technology trends, economic principles, and diverse political systems.
During my studies, I concentrated my elective coursework on Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence because I recognized their pivotal role in driving technological innovation and transforming approaches to solving complex real-world challenges.
